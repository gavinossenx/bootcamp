Integrity. Experience. Relationships. Professionalism

Those words describe Kelley Commercial, LLC. They also describe our commitment to every client, large and small.

Serving the commercial real estate industry since 2010, Kelley Commercial is a leader in vertically integrated development and management consulting services. We anticipate every challenge. And we provide the skills and experience needed to make every project a success.

Our longstanding relationships with many of the largest and most respected professionals in brokerage, design and construction allow us to create cost-effective solutions for all commercial real estate challenges.

WHO WE ARE

LEADERSHIP

Michael Kelley founded Kelley Commercial, LLC, in 2010. Mike brings more than two decades of experience at the highest levels of commercial real estate development and asset management.

Mike learned from the bottom up. He started with a small, family owned construction company. From there, he rose to senior positions at two of California’s largest and most respected commercial and vertically integrated real estate development and asset management firms – Panattoni Development Company and Buzz Oates.

From acquisition, design and entitlement through construction, asset management, marketing and financial reporting, Mike directs Kelley Commercial with the skill and knowledge that come from having developed and managed millions of square feet of commercial properties. Our diverse portfolios include neighborhood, regional and national projects.

Mike holds degrees from UCLA and UC Davis, where he earned his MBA in 2000. He has been a LEED Accredited Professional for New Construction since 2009, and is a specialist in energy efficient and environmentally responsible concepts.
